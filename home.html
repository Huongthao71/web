Hai Phong in my heart

Hai Phong

Coastal city of Hai Phong is located in the east gate of Red River Delta in a distance of 102 km to the Northeast from HaNoi Capital, borders with Quang Ninh in the North, Hai Duong in the West, Thai Binh in the South and Bac Bo gulf in the East. This is the thirst largest city of Vietnam (after Ha Noi capital and Ho Chi Minh city) and plays an important role in the socio economic development as well as in security and defense of the northern region and the whole country.
