Hai Phong in my heart

OUR PLACES

Do Son Beach

Do Son beach, 21km southeast of Hai Phong city, is covered with sandalwood trees and surrounded by mountains. The small beach can easily be completely submerged by the tide, but it's a really nice place to relax. Foreigners coming here can have fun in Vietnam's first casino at Do Son Resort Hotel opens in new window.

Lan Ha Bay

Lan Ha Bay is located to the east of Cat Ba Island and coadjacent to Ha Long Bay to the south. Owing to the unspoiled and quiet natural beauty, nowdays Lan Ha Bay has been put into operation as the most attractive resort destination for domestic and foreign tourists only after Ha Long Bay.

Thap Temple

Tuong Long Pagoda is located on the top of Long Son, Ngoc Xuyen ward, Hai Phong city. This is the first of nine mountains running along the Do Son peninsula. Tuong Long tower pagoda is not only a sacred place for tourists as well as people to visit on holidays but also a place. ideal place to visit as well as learn about the cultural and historical values of the nation.
