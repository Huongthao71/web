Hai Phong in my heart

OUR HISTORY

Hai Phong city is a province of Vietnam called the Hoa Phuong Do City. The land of Hai Phong was formed in association with the legend of female generals Le Chan following Trung Trac and Trung Nhi to expel the Eastern Han army during the French colonial period. She founded the village of Ven (An Bien) - the forerunner of owing to Hai Phong city. Experiencing many ups and downs of history with illustrious resistance wars against foreign invaders, with a typical land where the waves and winds are favored by nature with passionate patriotism. The courage, resilience, and creativity of a Hai Phong resident has created a dynamic and developing city.
